Proclaimed by the Free Peoples of the Earth

The Global Charter of Human Liberty

Founded upon Natural Law, Magna Carta, and the Inalienable Rights of All Persons

✦ ✦ ✦
We hold these truths to be self-evident: that all human beings, of every nation, tongue, and creed, are created equal — endowed with rights that no government, corporation, unelected body, or technological system may lawfully take from them. These rights are not granted by institutions. They precede them. They supersede them. Where any power becomes destructive of these ends, it is the right and duty of the people to resist, reform, and reclaim their sovereignty.
Part I — The Sovereignty of the Body & Mind
Article IBodily Sovereignty

No Forced Medical Testing, Vaccination, or Experimentation

The human body is sovereign territory. No government, corporation, international health authority, or military power may subject any person to medical testing, vaccination, drug administration, surgical procedure, or biological experimentation without that person's full, free, prior, and informed consent. Coercion through employment, travel, commerce, social participation, or financial penalty constitutes force and is hereby declared unlawful against free peoples.

Let it be further resolved: the deliberate release of experimental agents, pathogens, or biological material upon any population — including through insect vectors, aerosols, water supplies, or food systems — without the explicit, informed consent of that population constitutes an act of biological warfare and shall be prosecuted accordingly under universal law.

Article IICognitive Liberty

Freedom of Thought, Belief & Conscience

No person may be compelled to adopt any belief, religion, ideology, or worldview by force, coercion, indoctrination through public institutions, or social engineering. Parents hold the primary right and responsibility to educate their children according to their own values and conscience. No state, school system, or platform holds authority to supplant that bond. No religion, ideology, or creed may be imposed upon another — all people are free to believe, worship, question, and live by their own lights, so long as they do not impose upon others.

Part II — The Sovereignty of the Earth
Article IIIGeoengineering & Atmospheric Sovereignty

No Spraying, Geoengineering, or Atmospheric Manipulation

The atmosphere, skies, oceans, and soils of this Earth are the common inheritance of all living beings and generations yet unborn. No government, corporation, military, or international body holds unilateral authority to spray, seed, irradiate, ionize, or chemically alter the atmosphere, weather systems, or natural water cycles of this planet without the full, transparent, and publicly disclosed consent of the affected peoples. All geoengineering programs — including stratospheric aerosol injection, cloud seeding, and electromagnetic weather modification — must be immediately disclosed and subjected to free democratic review.

Any corporation or agency found responsible for deliberately introducing harmful substances into the air, water, or soil shall be subject to criminal prosecution, full restitution, and the removal of all operating licenses.

Article IVFood Sovereignty

The Inalienable Right to Natural Food

Access to clean, natural food is a fundamental human right. Every person on Earth has the inalienable right to grow vegetables, raise animals, fish, forage, and eat according to the nutritional traditions of their choosing. No government or corporation may criminalize the growing of food, the keeping of animals, or the choosing of diet. No person shall be coerced — through subsidy, regulation, social pressure, or financial exclusion — to consume insects, synthetic proteins, lab-cultured tissue, or any artificial substitute against their will.

The introduction of harmful chemicals, heavy metals, pharmaceutical agents, nano-materials, or undisclosed additives into food or water supplies without full public disclosure and free consent is hereby declared an act of violence against the people. Those responsible — in any corporation or regulatory agency — shall face criminal accountability.

Part III — The Sovereignty of the Voice
Article VFree Expression

Absolute Freedom of Speech & No Censorship

Speech is the first freedom, for without it no other freedom may be defended. No platform, no government agency, no content moderation body, no international standards committee holds the authority to silence the voice of free people. If one disagrees with speech, the remedy is more speech — debate, rebuttal, refutation — not deletion, deplatforming, or criminalization. The power to turn off a channel lies with the individual listener. It does not lie with the state or the corporation.

Article VIEqual Justice

Equal Law for All — No Differential Punishment

A crime is a crime. It is defined by the act committed and the harm caused — not by the perceived motive, the identity of the perpetrator, or their membership in any group. The law must be blind to race, religion, sex, political affiliation, and social status. Differential sentencing based on the presumed ideology behind an act — rather than the act itself — is a tool of division and political persecution. Justice must be uniform, equal, and blind. The manufacture of division between peoples through law is hereby recognized as a weapon of control and rejected.

Article VIIPersonal Liberty

Live and Let Live — Autonomy in All Personal Matters

No person or institution requires the permission of the state to love whom they love, marry whom they choose, or make decisions about their own body and life — provided they cause no harm to another. The affairs of personal life are not the business of government. Governments derive their authority from the protection of the people — not from the management of their private existence. The principle is ancient and universal: live and let live.

Part IV — The Sovereignty of Commerce & Governance
Article VIIIPolitical Corruption

No Corporate Donations to Public Officials

The corruption of representative government by private financial interests is the destruction of democracy by definition. No elected official, candidate for office, or political party shall accept financial contributions from corporations, political action committees, lobbying organizations, foreign entities, or any institution whose interests before the legislature constitute a conflict with the public good. The elected serve the people — not the donor class. Violations shall constitute grounds for immediate removal from office and criminal prosecution.

Article IXPolitical Accountability

Elected Officials Must Serve Their Platform — or Be Removed

An elected official who campaigns upon a stated platform and then, once in office, systematically betrays that platform in favor of other interests has obtained office by deception. The people hold the sovereign right — without interference from government, courts, or administrative agencies — to remove any elected official who betrays the mandate upon which they were elected. Government does not grant this right. Government does not restrict this right. The authority is the people's. It is inalienable.

Article XTransparency

No Classified Information Held Beyond Three Years

Governments that derive their just powers from the consent of the governed cannot govern by permanent secrecy. Any information classified in the name of national security must be automatically and fully declassified and released to the public no later than three years from the date of its classification. No extension, no exception, no executive override. The people have a right to know what is done in their name, with their resources, upon the world. Secrets held beyond this term are presumed evidence of wrongdoing against the public and shall be treated accordingly.

Article XIMonetary Sovereignty

No Private Institutions Running Government Monetary Policy

No private banking institution, central bank cartel, international monetary body, or corporate financial consortium shall hold the authority to create currency, set interest rates, generate sovereign debt, or determine the monetary policy of free nations. The creation of money is a function of the sovereign people — not of private profit-seeking entities. Governments that borrow from private institutions at interest, surrendering the monetary sovereignty of their people, do so against the interests and without the true consent of those people. Fiscal sanity, sound money, and debt-free public finance are fundamental rights of free nations.

Part V — The Prohibition of Existential Threats
Article XIIArtificial Intelligence

No AI Governance, No Artificial Gods

Artificial intelligence is a tool of human ingenuity and must remain subject to human authority, human ethics, and human law. No AI system shall be granted governing authority over free peoples. No algorithmic system shall determine the rights, freedoms, social standing, financial access, or physical liberty of any person without transparent human accountability and due process. The construction of autonomous AI systems designed to replace human governance, supplant human spiritual life, or function as objects of worship or ultimate authority — so-called artificial gods — is hereby declared incompatible with human civilization and freedom.

The digital enslavement of humanity through surveillance capitalism, programmable money, social credit scoring, and AI-controlled access to society must be recognized for what it is: the most sophisticated system of control ever devised. It shall be resisted by every peaceful and lawful means available to free people.

Article XIIIPeace

War Is Not the Answer — Leaders Must Bear Its Cost

War is the ultimate failure of civilization. It is the mechanism by which the powerful send the poor and hungry to die in conflicts manufactured for profit, territory, and dominance. There is no cause in the 21st century that justifies the mass slaughter of free peoples who bear no personal quarrel with one another. We declare that the era of industrial warfare must end. If any leader of any nation seeks to wage war upon another people, let that leader — and the opposing leader, together with their families — meet the challenge directly and personally, rather than dispatching the sons and daughters of the working poor to bleed in their place. No more.

The free peoples of the world demand the abolition of war as an instrument of national policy and the redirection of all military expenditure toward the eradication of disease, hunger, poverty, and the expansion of human civilization into a peaceful and flourishing future.

In witness whereof, the free peoples of the Earth do hereby affirm

We the People of the World
