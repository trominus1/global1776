A Modern Declaration of Rights for All Humanity
The Declaration

We the People of the World

We hold this truth as universal: all human beings are born equal in dignity, liberty, and natural rights. These rights are not granted by institutions. They precede them. They supersede them. Where any power becomes destructive of these ends, it is the right and duty of the people to peacefully resist, reform, and reclaim their sovereignty.

Articles of Liberty

A Charter Built for the Modern Age

Magna Carta restraint. Constitutional principle. Modern protection against digital control, coercion, censorship, corruption, and permanent secrecy.

I

Bodily Sovereignty

No forced medical testing, medication, vaccination, or experimentation without informed consent.

II

Free Speech

No censorship, compelled speech, or criminalization of conscience. The answer to speech is more speech.

III

Equal Law

Justice must be uniform, blind, and based on the act committed — not political classification.

IV

No Corporate Capture

No corporate PAC money or financial influence over public officials entrusted with the people’s power.

V

Public Transparency

No permanent secrecy. Classified information must face automatic public review and release.

VI

Food Freedom

Every person has the right to grow, raise, preserve, and choose real food without coercion.

VII

No Digital Shackles

Reject social credit, programmable control, biometric coercion, and AI-governed access to life.

VIII

Peace Over War

End permanent war and redirect human effort toward health, discovery, abundance, and civilization.

Peaceably. Lawfully. Together. Unstoppable.
The Future

The 21st Century Enlightenment

The purpose is not merely to oppose tyranny. The purpose is to build a civilization worthy of free people.

A Healthier Humanity

Clean food, clean water, medical consent, longer lives, and science aimed at healing instead of dependency.

Technology Under Human Authority

AI, automation, and advanced systems must serve human dignity — never replace human judgment or rule mankind.

Families, Faith, and Conscience

No person should be forced to accept a belief, ideology, religion, lifestyle, or medical choice imposed by institutions.

Exploration, Abundance, and Peace

Redirect the resources of permanent conflict toward discovery, space, innovation, and the flourishing of all peoples.
