The Four Pillars of Human Sovereignty

🕊

Liberty

The right to think, speak, believe, associate, and live freely without fear or censorship.

🤝

Consent

No one may be coerced in matters of body, mind, property, belief, data, medicine, or choice.

◉

Transparency

Power must be visible, information must be open, and truth must be allowed to prevail.

●

Human Sovereignty

Rights belong to individuals. No authority stands above the human being.
